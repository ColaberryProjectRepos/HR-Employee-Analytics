How to Download Qlik Sense 6

This step will guide you on how to download Qlik Sense and Vizlib so that you can start building Qlik Sense dashboards.

  •        Open Google Chrome in Incognito mode

     

            Type: https://colaberry.us.qlikcloud.com/  in the search window



            Email:  Qlik_user@colaberry.com

    •       Click the AM icon on the top right and choose Settings


  •        Scroll down to Qlik Sense Desktop


  •         Choose Download

  •            Next click Authenticate
