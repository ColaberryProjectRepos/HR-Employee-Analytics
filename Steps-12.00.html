Creating Filter pane

  • Click on Charts then select filter pane under the chart options.
  • Click on the bottom right corner to add the filter pane to the sheet
  • Click on dimension to add Gender.
  • After adding the first dimension you will get another button to add dimension under the first one.
  • Click on it and add MaritalDesc to the filter pane
  • Repeat the process to add ManagerName to the filter pane. Change the title to Manager.


Sheet title styling

Start off by clicking on the ellipses before the project title

Select orchid (#8e477d) for the left side of the title background

Select a lighter orchid (#a65392) for the right side of the title background

Select White for the font color

Then exit the box
