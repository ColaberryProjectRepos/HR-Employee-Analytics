Findings & Suggestions
  1. The company needs to put all employees on a Performance Improvement Plan. Only 13 people are on PIP but the data shows that 18 employees need improvement.

  3. Baby boomers have a high turnover rate and the least average months worked. 
  4. The Admin department employees have the shortest tenure with the company. The company needs to attract them to stay longer with the company. Maybe turnover will go down if the Admin is not changing all the time. 
  5. The Production Department has the highest  turnover rate of 40% and the Production Technician ? position has a turnover rate of 46%.
